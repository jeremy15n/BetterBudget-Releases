# BetterBudget - Refund Policy

**Last Updated: March 30, 2026**

## Overview

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase of BetterBudget. If the Software
does not work as expected, we offer refunds under the following terms.

## Eligibility

You may request a full refund within **14 days** of your original purchase date
if:
- The Software fails to install or activate on your system
- The Software does not function as described on our website
- You experience a technical issue that we are unable to resolve

## Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:
- The refund request is made more than 14 days after purchase
- You have violated the End User License Agreement (EULA)
- Your product key has been shared with or transferred to a third party
- You simply changed your mind after using the Software (the product key has
  already been activated and used)

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at **support@betterbudgetapp.com** with:
- Your name
- The email address used for purchase
- Your product key
- A brief description of the issue

We will review your request and respond within **5 business days**. Approved
refunds will be processed through Stripe to your original payment method.
Please allow **5-10 business days** for the refund to appear on your statement.

## Product Key Deactivation

Upon issuing a refund, your product key will be deactivated and your license
to use the Software will be terminated.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, contact: support@betterbudgetapp.com
